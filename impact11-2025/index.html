About the Retreat

This exclusive 3-day immersive experience is designed for the partners of Impact11 leaders. Guided by Jesus Hidalgo and Austin Mao, you'll embark on two sacred psilocybin ceremonies woven with experiential workshops, nourishing meals, and deep connection. Held in a luxury private estate in the Colorado foothills, every detail of the weekend is designed for intimacy, safety, and transformation.
