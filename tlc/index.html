Ceremonia

A relational practice for authentic communication, empathetic listening, and profound connection.

Circling fosters self-awareness and connection by exploring emotions and intentions in the moment. Through this practice, participants build trust, intimacy, and mutual understanding in a safe and supportive environment.

Core Principles

  1. Welcome Everything: Embrace all experiences and emotions without judgment.
  2. Assume Nothing: Approach interactions with curiosity and no assumptions.
  3. Stay with Sensation: Focus on bodily sensations to stay grounded in the present.
  4. Own Your Experience: Take responsibility for your feelings with honest "I" statements.
  5. Be With The Other: Empathize by deeply listening and understanding their world.
  6. Reveal Your Experience: Share your inner thoughts and feelings to deepen connection.
  7. Find the Essence: Seek the deeper truth in interactions and emotions.
  8. Allow Impact: Be open to others‚Äô effects on you and share authentically.
  9. Slow Down: Take time to be present and engage thoughtfully.
  10. Stay in Connection: Maintain connection, even in disagreement or discomfort.
